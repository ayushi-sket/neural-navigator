
#  Enterprise AI Goal Deconstructor: User Guide

A scalable, modular MERN platform powered by the **Google Gemini 2.5 Flash API**. This guide details how to navigate the visual dashboard elements to turn high-level ideas into actionable plans.

---

##  App Interface Features

* **Dynamic Focus Banner**  
  Greets you at the top of the screen with an AI-generated motivational focus spark that refreshes automatically on every session.
* **Smart Parameter Input Matrix**  
  Captures your primary target metrics and automatically adjusts the layout fields if you toggle financial variables or collaborative team panels.
* **Interactive Milestone Roadmap**  
  Renders your strategic task timeline with dedicated visual badges for individual time markers, role assignments, and gamified celebration alerts.
* **On-Demand Progress Review**  
  Features an inline tracking mechanism that hooks your checkbox percentages directly into an AI analyst to get real-time performance feedback.

---

##  Step-by-Step UI Browser Guide

### 1. Configure Your Project Parameters
1. **Define Your Objective**: Type a clear long-term target into the text input box (e.g., *Launch e-commerce fashion storefront*, *Train for half marathon*).
2. **Set Your Energy Constraints**: Enter your available allocation in the `Hours/Week` field.
3. **Map Your Deadline**: Input a number in the `Duration Value` slot and select **Days**, **Weeks**, or **Months** from the dropdown menu to set the timeline scale.

### 2. Activate Advanced Data Add-ons (Optional)
* **Financial Tracking**: Tick this box to unlock a budget constraint input. Gemini will factor cost-efficiency directly into the roadmap steps.
* **Team Assignment**: Tick this box and type active roles separated by commas (e.g., *Designer, DevOps, Marketer*). Gemini will automatically distribute tasks to specific owners across milestones.

### 3. Generate and Execute Your Blueprint
1. Click the blue **Generate Advanced Blueprint** button. The button text will toggle to show a loading phase while the backend parses the JSON data.
2. Once loaded, scroll down to see your roadmap cards. Review the custom **Reward Unlock Target** generated for each milestone to keep your habits engaged.
3. As you complete steps, tick the checkboxes. A high-performance **canvas-confetti screen animation blast** will trigger instantly, and your completion state saves to your database.

### 4. Trigger an On-Demand AI Performance Review
1. Once you finish structural sub-tasks within a specific phase, click the black **Request AI Evaluation** button on that milestone block.
2. The UI will instantly check your data metrics. The AI analyst box will expand directly below the list to write a tailored, two-sentence strategic evaluation based entirely on your literal progress.
